A New Way To Reach Inbox Zero

MysticMail is a brand new conceptual mail app that will revolutionize the way you think about your inbox.

All Your Accounts

Send and receive emails for all your accounts in one place. You can add up to 100 accounts, each with its own inbox, calendar, and contacts list.

Custom Email Templates

Designed to save you time with custom templates for messages that you send on a regular basis. Select the template, make a few changes, then send. It's that easy.

Events and Tasks

The built-in calendar and task list integrates seamlessly with your existing calendar and task app so you never miss a beat.
